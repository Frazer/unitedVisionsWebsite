United Visions Foundation Inc.

United Visions is a nonprofit organization created to empower people at every stage of life to hit the restart button and realign their sense of reality, direction, and purpose. Based in Sunnyvale, California, our global lifestyle movement offers content, workshops, and 1:1 coaching to teach various forms of brain programming and meditation to recognize and reset our faulty defaults. Our goal is to apply the art and science of brain plasticity to help people with the habits and attitudes to live life with joy, ease and success, as well as be able to cope more effectively with whatever life throws at them. In doing so, we hope to eliminate the plagues of our times and usher in a new era of peace, love, and unity.

About Us

Frazer Kirkman - Founder

As a young man, I had a vision that changed my life forever. After a tragic incident in my family, it hit me that the world does not have to be the way it is right now, that we can help each other be happier, wiser, and more caring.

I dedicated myself to making the world the way it could be, or as I phrased it at the time, the way it is meant to be. A world where every single person has access to the best training to be happy, to be positive, and to have peace. But more importantly than that, I want to train people with the skills to be the designers of themselves and our communities.

Marlis Kirkman - Founder

Marlis firmly believes that people can do better with their brains. She's dedicated to educating everyone about the incredible adaptability of our brains and won't rest until everyone knows. Kind and radical change is her expertise.
